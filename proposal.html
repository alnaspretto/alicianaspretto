Website Proposal: Naspretto Alicia

Purpose and Goals

Alicia Naspretto is a graphic and web designer with experience building websites for non-profits in central Texas. In addition, she’s worked to highlight their impact through her videography and photography work. Examples of this can all be found at www.alicianaspretto.com.

Target Audience

The target audience for www.alicianaspretto.com. will hopefully be future employers or clients. I plan to post this URL to my LinkedIn and have it on my resume. I hope to have different pages on my website that highlight the different skills I can offer.
One page can have screenshots or links to web pages I’ve created that I’m proud of. Another page can have a slideshow of pictures I’ve taken. A third page will show some of my graphic design work and one more for my video work. Finally I hope to have a page with my actual resume or work experience listed.

Competitor Analysis with Examples

I actually already have a website like the one I am looking to build, however I designed it through GoDaddy instead of building it myself. I also know a few other people who have similar portfolio websites, but again I don’t know anyone who’s built it themselves. I think the fact that mine will be built by me instead of just designed by me gives me more credibility for working on websites.
  • https://alicianaspretto.godaddysites.com/ - this is my current site. I like the colors and the set up of it, but again would really like to have built it myself. I would like to add more to the homepage and also find a different way to display my graphic design work.
  • https://www.sydneyzatz.com/ - this is a portfolio page for a journalist I know. (As a former journalist myself, most of the portfolio pages I know are for other journalists) I don’t really like the set up of her page because when you click the tabs across the top, it doesn’t bring you to a new page, it simply brings you down the homepage to whichever section you’re wanting to see. Since my work is all very different, I want it all on completely different pages.
  • https://www.elle-meyers.com/ - this website is set up a lot better as far as having different pages for each tab, however there is no color anywhere on her page. My current portfolio color is currently yellow and white, which I might carry over to the new one or come up with a new color theme.


Content Strategy

As mentioned above, the visual content on my page will be the work I’ve created as far as examples of my graphic and web design, photography and videography. I will accompany some of these with captions. More written content will be my work experience and a contact me section.

Navigation

My page will definitely be more than one page, but I’m not sure exactly how many yet. I would like a homepage introducing the viewer to me and my work. This page will consist of my headshot, a bio, contact info and a few other things to fill it out. Then I would like a "Graphic Design" page that will display some of the graphics I have made, a "Web Design" page to show some of that work, a "Photography" page, a "Videography" page, "Experience" to highlight my resume and I might have a few more if I think of them but this is what I’m think right now.

Font Families

For the fonts, I chose to use Playwrite Cursive as my header and Playfair Display, serif, as my main text.
